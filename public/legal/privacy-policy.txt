Privacy Policy for CSM Issue Management Dashboard

1. Data Collection
We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services, including your name, campus email, and campus ID. This data is used solely for the purpose of managing and resolving reported issues.

2. Media Uploads
Photos and videos uploaded to the platform are used exclusively by the staff to identify and resolve campus issues. We do not use this media for any other purposes.

3. Data Security
Your personal information is stored securely and is encrypted. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or external organizations.

4. Account Management
You have the right to view your data and request account deletion. Session data can be managed through your profile settings.

5. Policy Updates
We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the dashboard.
